friendblur

A quick game of friendly recognition

Sign in with Facebook and Play

Friendblur tests how well you can recognize your friends, based on their Facebook profile picture. You will be given 16 seconds to identify three of your friends. Think you can handle it?

Info Because of the Facebook profiles, Friendblur requires that you sign-in using Facebook. Friendblur does not save any of your information — there's no database, even.

This game uses some newer browser features, so it probably works best on newer versions of Chrome, Firefox and Internet Explorer.
